# Project1
Game AI Project Formation Clash

A tactical battle simulator based on games like Clash Royale and Pokemon. The basic idea is that there's a pool of units and each level has a fixed enemy lineup. You get to see the opponent’s selection, then choose your own units to fight against them. Once the battle starts, the units fight on their own based on their AI, and you win or lose depending on how well you chose the units and where you placed them on the map.
Each unit has two properties that work in a rock-paper-scissors style. One property is: melee, ranged, or flying and the other is attack pattern: single target, team, or AoE. So you might have a ranged unit that attacks in an AoE, or a flying unit that focuses on single enemies.
